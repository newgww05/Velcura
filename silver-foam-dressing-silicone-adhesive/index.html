Advanced Wound Dressing

Velcura® Silver Foam Dressing Silicone Adhesive

Silver Foam Dressing with Silicone Adhesive combines antimicrobial silver ions with absorbent silicone foam to manage light to heavy exudate wounds. Silver ions help reduce infection risk and support faster healing, while the silicone foam layer absorbs exudate and maintains a moist wound healing environment for optimal tissue repair.

  • Antimicrobial silver protects against infection

  • Absorbs light to heavy exudate

  • Maintains moist healing balance

  • Silicone adhesive for secure, gentle fixation
